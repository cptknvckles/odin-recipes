Today's featured recipe is pancakes! everyone loves them

Perfectly soft and fluffy Cinnamon Pancakes with Homemade Cinnamon Syrup is soon to be your new favorite breakfast recipe that will warm you from the inside out!

You will need

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tspns baking powder
  • 1/2 tspns salt
  • 1 tblsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tblsp butter melted

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl sift together the flour, bakin powder, and sugar
  2. make a well in the middle and pour milk, egg and butter and whisk until smooth
  3. heat a non stick griddle or large skillet over medium high heat
  4. pour out 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake, wait for bubbles then flip
  5. ENJOY
