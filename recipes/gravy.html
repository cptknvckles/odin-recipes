How to make gravy

Everyone loves gravy!

Making homemade gravy is an important kitchen skill for any home cook.
You can easily and quickly whip up a homemade gravy to accompany any meal.
Make gravy for mashed potatoes, turkey, and pot roast or whip up
some simple sausage gravy to serve over biscuits or toast

Ingredients

  • 2 tbls butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour(or 2tbls cornstarch)
  • 2cups chicken,beef,turkey or veggie broth

Instructions

  1. In a medium sauce pan melt butter over medium high heat
  2. whisk in flour until well combined and no white specks, cook 2min
  3. slowly pour in broth and whisk well
  4. bring to simmer and heat until thickened to desired consistency<
    whisking constantly

And there you go! You know have gravy for any occasion
